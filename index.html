Learn about Endangered Animals!

What are Endangered Animals?

Endangered Animals or endangered species are species of different animals that are very likely to go extinct in the near future.

How did this happen?

Animals started to become extinct due to the climate, having competition with other species, having to fight for food, hunters, and loss of habitats.

Effects of what happened.

Considering different ecosystems depends on certain animals, they are starting to slowly die off considering the animals are also dying off.

Turtles!

  • Can grow up to 2ft long and weigh up to 100-300lbs!
  • Often lives in open waters and coasts!
  • Killed due to human's demand for eggs, skin, meat, and shells.
  • There are only 3 Yangtze giant softshell turtle left in the world!
  • Average turtle lifespan ranges from 25-100+ years!

Rhinos!

  • Average height of a Rhino is 4'8ft - 6ft!
  • Average weight of a Rhino is around 1,500lbs - 8,000lbs!
  • There are 5 differnt Rhino species!

  • Average lifespan of a Rhino is between 35-50 years!
